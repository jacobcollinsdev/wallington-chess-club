Welcome to Wallington Chess Club

About Us

Wallington & Carshalton Chess Club was founded in 1895. It has been a constant member of the Surrey County Chess Association and has entered teams in the Surrey League for many years.

We have five teams in competitive leagues and an internal club championship. Our adult and junior club nights are Wednesday evenings, and we welcome players of all ages and abilities.

The club is open all Wednesdays except for all of August when it is closed, and for the last two weeks in December when it is closed.

Get Involved

Adults

Our adult session takes place every Wednesday from 19:30 - 22:00.

Juniors

We have two junior sessions aimed for those in Year 5 - Year 11 which run every Wednesday during term time only.

Session 1: 18:15 - 19:15.
Session 2: 19:30 - 20:30.

For more information about the Junior chess club contact Neill Cooper: junior@wallingtonchess.co.uk.
