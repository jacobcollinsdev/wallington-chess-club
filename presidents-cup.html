President's Cup

The President's Cup is the name given to the main internal Club Championship competition. The games played in this competition are graded and count towards a member's English Chess Federation grade.

Time controls:
Default: 75 minutes + 10 secs/move.
Alternative: 60 minutes + 20 minutes after 30 moves (if agreed by both players).
